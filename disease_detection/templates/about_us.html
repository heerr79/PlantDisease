About CropFusion

Our Mission

CropFusion is dedicated to helping gardeners, farmers, and plant enthusiasts identify and treat plant diseases quickly and accurately. By leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning, we aim to make plant disease diagnosis accessible to everyone, promoting healthier plants and sustainable agricultural practices.

How It Works

Our system uses a deep learning model trained on thousands of images of healthy and diseased plants. When you upload a photo of your plant, our AI analyzes it and compares it against its database to identify potential diseases. The system then provides you with a diagnosis and customized care recommendations based on the detected condition and your local region.
